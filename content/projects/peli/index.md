## About

Peli is a platform built by four computer science students who share a passion for video games and software development. We created Peli to make it easy for gamers to stay up to date with upcoming game releases and keep track of the titles they're most excited about.

Our goal is to bring all upcoming releases into one simple, user-friendly place. With Peli, you can explore the latest game announcements, search for specific titles, and discover what's coming next across different platforms and genres.

By creating an account, users can personalize their experience—log in, favorite games they're looking forward to, and build their own list of upcoming releases to watch. Whether you're waiting for a major AAA launch or a hidden indie gem, Peli helps you stay informed and organized.

Peli is a student-built project driven by curiosity, creativity, and a love for gaming. We're constantly learning and improving, and we hope Peli becomes a useful companion for gamers who don't want to miss what's next.
